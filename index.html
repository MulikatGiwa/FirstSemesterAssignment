Biography

Mulikat Giwa is a lady in her late twenties from Lagos State, Nigeria.

She enjoys taking pictures of nature and spending time with friends and family.

She currently lives with her 2 year old daughter and husband in Saudi Arabia.

She has a bachelors degree in Architecture and a Masters degree in Environmental Design from University of Lagos, Nigeria.

She has years of experience working as an Architectural designer in Lagos, Nigeria before relocating with her family.

She has switched her career path and is currently studing Front End Engineering at Altschool Arica.

Why Altschool?

The question should be 'Why not Altchool?'

Mulikat Giwa heard about Altschool Africa from a friend of hers who is also a front end engineering student of Altschool.

Prior to this, she had always considered a switch to the tech industry given her young family and limited oppotunities as a foreign Architectural designer in Saudi Arabia.

She had come across various courses and schools but non really looked like they would give the foundation she hoped to build while switching to a different career path.

On learning about how structured and information packed the programme at Altschool Africa was she decided to enroll in the school of Engineering programme.
